# Echo Chamber — Characters

<!-- fact_id: fact_pkm_novel_chars_001 -->

Master list. New entries go at the bottom.

## Mira Voss (protagonist)

Late 30s, archivist. Skeptical, methodical. Defining trait: she edits
her own memories more than she realizes.
Voice: tight, dry, prone to footnotes.

## Theo Lan

Mira's ex. Soft-spoken, evasive. The one who built the echo service.
Whether he's a villain or a romantic lead is the spine of Act II.

## Captain Reyes

Police investigator who keeps showing up. Initially a skeptic of the
echo service, slowly drawn in. Used to be Mira's neighbor.

## Mira's mother (Hana)

Dies before Act I but appears in echoes. Big subtext for the climax.
