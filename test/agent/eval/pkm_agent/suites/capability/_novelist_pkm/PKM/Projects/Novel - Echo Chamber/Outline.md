# Echo Chamber — Outline

<!-- fact_id: fact_pkm_novel_outline_001 -->

Working title. Speculative fiction, near-future.

## Premise

A world where every conversation is replayed by an "echo" service that
reconstructs alternative versions of what the speaker *might have* said.

## Three-act spine

- Act I: Mira encounters her own echo from a fight 6 months ago, and
  it apologizes in a way she never did.
- Act II: Mira pursues whoever's behind the service. She finds it's
  a former lover. Their reunion reframes everything.
- Act III: She has to decide whether to keep her own real memories or
  let the echoes win.
