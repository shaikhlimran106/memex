# Reading

A running log of books I've finished and what actually stuck.

## The Pragmatic Programmer — Andrew Hunt & David Thomas
- Finished 2026-04-28, rated 4/5.
- Good reminders more than new ideas at this point, but the "broken windows"
  framing earned its keep.

## Designing Data-Intensive Applications — Martin Kleppmann
- Finished 2026-06-08, rated 5/5.
- Dense, took about two months. The replication and consistency chapters were
  the most useful — finally have a mental model for the tradeoffs.
- Want to revisit the stream processing chapter later.
